Conversation Overview

Steve Schneider (SMS) is a SUNY faculty member and AI Fellows for the Public Good participant developing an AI literacy curriculum framework. He is building a suite of interconnected documents including the AI-FYE (AI in the First Year Experience) faculty invitation, a Learner's Permit orientation scaffold, and a Commercial Driver's License framework. Steve works primarily through voice/audio input, which means his prompts are conversational and sometimes incomplete mid-thought; Claude is expected to track context carefully and reconstruct intent from fragments.
